Changa

Changa is a typeface designed by Argentinian type designer Eduardo Tunni. It was developed to be a display typeface that could be compatible with arabic scripts.

Letterform Analysis

The typeface was designed with a singular height so it could be used with minimal line spacing. Each letter was designed around a square, creating a contrast between the interior shapes and the curved contours. This constrast is part of what drew my eye to the typeface in the first place.
